# SAP-2025 Automation System 🚀

## 📌 Overview
The **SAP-2025 Automation System** is a web-based platform developed to automate the **Faculty Performance Management process** within an educational institution.  The system enables faculty members to record and submit their yearly academic activities such as **teaching workload, research publications, patents, and self-development programs** .It also provides a structured **multi-level approval workflow** where submitted reports are reviewed by **HoD, Principal, Director, and Chairman** before final approval . Additionally, the system generates **institutional analytics dashboards** that help administrators evaluate faculty productivity and research performance across departments and schools .

---

## ✨ Key Features
- 🧑‍🏫 Faculty performance record creation for each academic year  
- 📖 Teaching activity management  
- 🔬 Research and patent record tracking  
- 🎓 Self-development activity logging (FDPs, workshops, certifications)  
- 📂 Secure document upload with validation  
- 🏢 Multi-level approval workflow (HoD → Principal → Director → Chairman)  
- 📊 Institutional analytics dashboard for faculty and research performance  
- 📑 Automated report generation in **PDF and Excel formats**  
- 🔐 Role-based access control for secure system usage  
- 📝 Audit logging for transparency and activity tracking  

---

## 🧩 System Modules
The system is divided into several modules to handle different functionalities:

- 🔐 **Authentication Module** – Handles user registration, login, and role-based access  
- 🖥 **Faculty Dashboard** – Displays faculty progress, performance data, and submission status  
- 📚 **Teaching Module** – Manages teaching workload and academic activities  
- 🔬 **Research Module** – Records research papers, patents, and calculates research scores  
- 🎓 **Self Development Module** – Tracks workshops, FDPs, certifications, and training programs  
- 📂 **Document Upload Module** – Uploads and validates supporting documents  
- 📤 **Submission Module** – Ensures all records are complete before submission  
- 🧑‍💼 **Approval Modules** – Enables HoD, Principal, Director, and Chairman to review reports  
- 📊 **Analytics Engine** – Generates performance metrics and institutional insights  
- 📑 **Reporting Module** – Generates downloadable faculty reports and analytics reports
